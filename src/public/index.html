Basics

Cobrust is a multi-player online Snake game. Pick-up food on the map to grow, dodge other player's snake and use perks to create plays!

Use the create tab to customize settings such as the size, speed or the strength of foods. Once created, other players will be able to join your game using the games tab.

Move your snake using the WASD or the arrows keys of your keyboard.

Perks
Basic food, take it to grow.
Reserved food, take two foods in a row to spawn it, extra growth but deniable.
Reverser, take it to swap your head and tail.
Teleporter, take it to teleport to the other gate.
Speed boost, take it to temporary double your speed.
Tips
While in the lobby, double click a game to join.
